**Core Aesthetic**: Islamic manuscript elegance meets Apple-level clarity. Think **Al-Azhar library illuminated by daylight** â€” scholarly, reverent, but accessible.

**Color Psychology**:
- Emerald green = Knowledge, growth, Islamic tradition
- Gold = Divine light, truth, manuscript illumination
- Cream = Parchment, warmth, readability
- Burgundy = Urgency (for corrections/warnings)

**Typography Hierarchy**:
- Display (hero text): 72px / Bold
- Headings: 48px, 36px, 28px
- Body: 18px (readability > minimalism)
- Arabic: Always 20-30% larger than English equivalent
