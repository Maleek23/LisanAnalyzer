# Lisan - Landing Page, Homepage & Account Management Design Spec

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

**Core Aesthetic**: Islamic manuscript elegance meets Apple-level clarity. Think **Al-Azhar library illuminated by daylight** — scholarly, reverent, but accessible.

**Color Psychology**:
- Emerald green = Knowledge, growth, Islamic tradition
- Gold = Divine light, truth, manuscript illumination
- Cream = Parchment, warmth, readability
- Burgundy = Urgency (for corrections/warnings)

**Typography Hierarchy**:
- Display (hero text): 72px / Bold
- Headings: 48px, 36px, 28px
- Body: 18px (readability > minimalism)
- Arabic: Always 20-30% larger than English equivalent

## 👤 Account Management System

### **Account Creation Flow** (Privacy-First)

#### **Option 1: No Account (Default)**
- All features work **without login**
- Saves recent searches in **localStorage** (private, never leaves device)
- Banner: "Create account to save analyses across devices"

#### **Option 2: Email-Only Account**

**What We DON'T Ask For**:
- ❌ Full name (not needed)
- ❌ Phone number (privacy risk)
- ❌ Country/location (tracks users)
- ❌ Profile photo (optional, added later)
- ❌ Social media links (not relevant)

**Authentication**: 
- Email + password (bcrypt hashed)
- Magic link option (passwordless, click email link to sign in)
- 2FA optional (TOTP for scholars)
